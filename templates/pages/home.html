District School Performance Predictor

Created by the Educational Leadership Research Center (ELRC) at Texas A&M University, this software as a service computes student STAAR test scores that enables users to make informed decisions during the critical time periods between final benchmark tests and official STAAR tests.

With the predictions computed by the District School Performance Predictor machine learning model, userâ€™s can determine which core subject areas their school needs improvement in, specific students that need help, an estimate on how far a student is to the next Texas Education Agency (TEA) performance categorization, and can provide an advanced way to numerically assure parents their children are being prepared for their next grade level.
