District School Performance Predictor

Created by the Educational Leadership Research Center (ELRC) at Texas A&M University, this software as a service generates student STAAR test score predictions that enables users to make informed decisions during critical time periods between benchmark tests and official STAAR tests.

With the predictions computed by the District School Performance Predictor (DSPP) machine learning model, userâ€™s can determine which core subject areas their school needs improvement in, specific students that need help, an estimate on how far a student is to the next Texas Education Agency (TEA) performance categorization, and can provide an advanced way to numerically assure parents their children are being prepared for their next grade level.

To utilize this service start by registering an account and then proceed to the How to Create an Information File.
