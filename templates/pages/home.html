Welcome To The

District School Performance Predictor

Created by the Educational Leadership Research Center (ELRC) at Texas A&M University this software as a service provides school leaders with accurate student STAAR assessments score predictions in order to enable school leaders to make informed decisions on where to focus school resources, determine which core subject areas need improvement, and an advanced way to provide parents assurance that their children are prepared for the next grade level.

To utilize this service start by registering an account and then proceed to the How to Create an Information File.
